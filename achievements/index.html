Achieve­ments

Media & Awards

Glob­al Patent Recog­ni­tions

Jen­do has been rec­og­nized glob­al­ly for its inno­v­a­tive approach to vas­cu­lar health mon­i­tor­ing through pres­ti­gious patents award­ed in mul­ti­ple coun­tries. These patents include recog­ni­tion from the Unit­ed States, Japan, and Sri Lan­ka, affirm­ing the unique­ness and impact of our tech­nol­o­gy in the field of non-inva­sive car­dio­vas­cu­lar health diag­nos­tics. Each patent under­scores our com­mit­ment to ground­break­ing research, tech­no­log­i­cal advance­ment, and ensur­ing intel­lec­tu­al prop­er­ty rights as we expand our pres­ence across the globe.

ICT Leader of the Year 2025 — Nation­al ICT Awards  

Rec­og­nized as one of the high­est nation­al hon­ors in Sri Lanka’s infor­ma­tion and com­mu­ni­ca­tion tech­nol­o­gy sec­tor, this award rep­re­sents a sig­nif­i­cant pro­fes­sion­al mile­stone. Con­ferred annu­al­ly by the Com­put­er Soci­ety of Sri Lan­ka (CSSL), it acknowl­edges excep­tion­al lead­er­ship, sus­tained excel­lence, and mean­ing­ful con­tri­bu­tions to the advance­ment of the ICT indus­try. Achieved at the age of 35, this recog­ni­tion also places the recip­i­ent among the youngest awardees in the his­to­ry of the hon­or, reflect­ing a strong com­mit­ment to inno­va­tion, strate­gic lead­er­ship, and nation­al impact.

The achieve­ment has received wide­spread recog­ni­tion across promi­nent nation­al insti­tu­tions and media plat­forms, includ­ing the Com­put­er Soci­ety of Sri Lan­ka, Dai­ly Mir­ror, Sun­day Times, and the Min­istry of Dig­i­tal Econ­o­my, fur­ther affirm­ing its impor­tance with­in Sri Lanka’s evolv­ing dig­i­tal and inno­va­tion ecosys­tem.

Young Inno­va­tor of the Year Award 2024  

Rec­og­nized as Young Inno­va­tor of the Year by the Fac­ul­ty of Allied Health Sci­ences at the Uni­ver­si­ty of Sri Jayewar­denepu­ra, this award acknowl­edges lead­er­ship in med-tech inno­va­tion and mean­ing­ful con­tri­bu­tions toward advanc­ing mod­ern health­care.

The recog­ni­tion high­lights the devel­op­ment and lead­er­ship of pio­neer­ing solu­tions that effec­tive­ly bridge tech­nol­o­gy and health­care, improv­ing acces­si­bil­i­ty, diag­nos­tics, and patient out­comes. It also reflects the grow­ing impor­tance of inno­va­tion in address­ing crit­i­cal chal­lenges with­in the med­ical and allied health sec­tors.

This hon­or is both hum­bling and moti­vat­ing, rein­forc­ing a con­tin­ued com­mit­ment to dri­ving trans­for­ma­tive health­care advance­ments and sup­port­ing the broad­er mis­sion of improv­ing lives through tech­nol­o­gy.

CEO of the Year Award 2022 — Arti­fi­cial Intel­li­gence Cat­e­go­ry  

Award­ed CEO of the Year – Arti­fi­cial Intel­li­gence Cat­e­go­ry at the Nation­al CEO Awards Night orga­nized by The CEO Mag­a­zine, this recog­ni­tion hon­ors lead­er­ship in lever­ag­ing arti­fi­cial intel­li­gence to dri­ve inno­va­tion, oper­a­tional excel­lence, and mean­ing­ful soci­etal impact.

The award reflects the col­lec­tive efforts of a high-per­form­ing team com­mit­ted to apply­ing AI tech­nolo­gies to solve com­plex chal­lenges and deliv­er real-world val­ue. Appre­ci­a­tion is extend­ed to The CEO Mag­a­zine and the dis­tin­guished pan­el of judges for this hon­or, which rein­forces a con­tin­ued com­mit­ment to vision­ary lead­er­ship and advanc­ing inno­va­tion in the AI domain.
